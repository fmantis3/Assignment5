CALLA LILY

Definition: either of two plants of the arum family.

Three facts about Calla Lilies

  • Though called a lily, Calla lilies are not really lilies.
  • Calla is a flowering genus of twenty-eight species
    originating in Africa and growing mostly in marshy areas.
  • Calla lilies are grown from bulbous roots with finger-like growths.
