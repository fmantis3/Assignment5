SUNFLOWERS

Definition: a prickly bush or shrub that typically bears red, pink, yellow,
or white fragrant flowers, native to north temperate regions. Numerous
hybrids and cultivars have been developed and are widely grown
as ornamentals.

Three facts about Sunflowers

  • They are native to the Americas
  • Sunflowers move with the Sun
